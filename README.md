# 🇮🇳 SchemeSaathi – Government Schemes Made Easy

SchemeSaathi is a user-friendly web application designed to simplify access to Indian government schemes. It helps users quickly find, understand, and compare schemes based on their life situation.

## 🚀 Features

- 📋 View all major government schemes in one place
- 🧭 "My Situation" filter (job loss, student, farmer, etc.)
- ✅ Quick eligibility checker (Yes/No questions)
- ⚖️ Compare multiple schemes side-by-side
- 🌐 Language toggle (Hindi/English)
- 🧠 Simple mode for easy understanding
- 🔊 Voice support for accessibility
- 📄 Download & share scheme details

## 💡 Problem Solved

Many people are unaware of government schemes or find them too complex. SchemeSaathi removes confusion by presenting schemes in simple language with real-life examples.
